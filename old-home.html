Ingredients

This moisturizer/serum hybrid instantly hydrates skin like a tall glass of water. A deeply hydrating serum infused with bakuchiol & hyaluronic acid that plumps skin & delivers visible improvement in pores, texture, & wrinkles.

Apply 2-3 pumps on clean skin, after toning. Massage and let absorb into the skin. SLUSHY can be used as a serum, moisturizer or both.

Bakuchiol: Known as a plant-based retinol alternative that helps refine pores, smooth the look of fine lines, uneven texture & calm the look of breakouts.

‍Hyaluronic Acid: a super hydrator, holds 1000x it’s weight in water. Plumps skin for a dewy, gorgeous bounce.

‍Yuzu: high in vitamin C & anti-oxidant properties, visibly brightens skin.
