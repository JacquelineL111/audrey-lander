Ingredients

This bundle includes 3 products to achieve healthy, gorgeous, glazed skin:

‍Mystery Glaze Lip Oil: Apply on its own or on top of your fave lipstick for a gorgeous glossy pout. Apply a 2nd coat for EXPLOSIVE SHINE!

‍Face Glaze - our favorite multi-use product:

- Apply as a primer for all over glow under foundation.
- Mix with your fave foundation or tinted moisturizer for a dewy, soft focus glow.
- Wear as a highlighter to add brightness to high points of your face.
- Mix a tiny bit with your eye cream for a brighter undereye.
